# Privacy Policy

StratumLister is a static, open-source documentation site. We prioritize absolute transparency and zero tracking.

## 1. Zero Data Collection
We do not use accounts, forms, comments, trackers, or cookies. We have no backend to collect or store your personal data.

## 2. Local Storage
We use your browser's `localStorage` to save your theme (light/dark) and your Stratum Bookmarks. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible by us.

## 3. Third-Party Resources
To keep the site fast and functional, we load some dependencies from third-party networks (jsDelivr, cdnjs, Google Fonts). When your browser requests these, those providers may see your technical metadata (IP address, user-agent) according to their own policies.

## 4. External Links
We link to many outside projects and tools. Once you leave Stratum, the destination site's privacy policy applies.

## 5. Open Source Transparency
You can audit every line of this website's code at any time:
[https://github.com/44tl/StratumLister](https://github.com/44tl/StratumLister)
