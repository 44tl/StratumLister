# Privacy Policy

StratumLister is a static, open-source documentation site with zero tracking.

## 1. No Data Collection

We do not use accounts, forms, comments, trackers, or cookies. We have no backend to collect or store your personal data.

## 2. Local Storage

We use your browser's `localStorage` to save your Stratum Bookmarks and recent pages. This data never leaves your device.

## 3. Third-Party Resources

The site loads dependencies from jsDelivr CDN. When your browser requests these, the provider may see standard technical metadata (IP address, user-agent).

## 4. External Links

We link to external projects. Once you leave Stratum, the destination site's privacy policy applies.
