# iOS Device Picker

Pick an iPhone or iPad by update support, battery condition, repair status, storage, and whether you need jailbreak compatibility.

## Quick Picks

| Use case | Pick by | Why |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Normal iPhone | Newest model you can afford with enough storage | Long update runway and better resale value. |
| Budget iPhone | Still-supported model on the latest iOS path | Avoid devices stuck on security-only updates if this is your main phone. |
| Used iPhone | Battery, parts history, activation status, carrier lock, update path | A cheap locked or repaired phone can become expensive fast. |
| iPad for school | Current iPadOS support, storage, Pencil/keyboard needs | App support and accessories matter more than raw benchmark scores. |
| Jailbreak testing | Spare A8-A11 device or a device already on a supported iOS 15/16 version | Modern iPhones on current iOS are not general jailbreak targets. |
| High-security use | Current iPhone on latest iOS with no jailbreak | Lockdown Mode and current patches matter more than customization. |

## Current Support Notes

- Apple lists iOS 26.4.1 for iPhone 11 and later.
- iOS 18.7.7 is still available for many older devices including iPhone XR, XS, XS Max, iPhone 11 through iPhone 16 models, and several iPads.
- Recent older-device releases include iOS 16.7.15 and iOS 15.8.7 for devices that cannot run current iOS.
- Security-only updates are useful, but a device outside the main iOS line should not be your long-term primary phone if you handle sensitive accounts.

## Used iPhone Checklist

Before buying:

- Check Settings > General > About for model, iOS version, serial, carrier lock, and parts/service history.
- Check battery health and cycle count if available.
- Confirm Face ID or Touch ID works.
- Confirm cameras, speakers, microphones, buttons, haptics, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, GPS, cellular, NFC, and charging work.
- Confirm Find My is off and Activation Lock is removed before payment.
- Confirm the seller can erase the phone and set it up to the Hello screen.
- Avoid devices with MDM, remote management, unknown parts, carrier financing issues, or "iCloud bypass" language.

## Storage Choice

| Storage | Good for | Avoid when |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 64 GB | Light use, streaming, minimal photos | You record video, keep offline media, or use many apps. |
| 128 GB | Basic modern default | You shoot lots of ProRes, games, or offline maps/media. |
| 256 GB | Best default for long ownership | You need professional video workflows. |
| 512 GB+ | Heavy media, travel, video, local files | Budget matters more than local storage. |

## Jailbreak Buying Rules

- Do not buy a new iPhone expecting a current public jailbreak.
- For palera1n, check A8-A11 device compatibility, not just iOS version.
- For Dopamine, check the exact iOS version and chip family.
- For TrollStore, check exact iOS version; 17.0.1 and newer are not supported by the official project.
- Never pay extra for vague "jailbreak ready" listings without screenshots of Settings > General > About.
