# iOS Setup Checklist

Use this after buying, factory resetting, or moving to a new iPhone or iPad.

## Before Setup

- Back up the old device.
- Save password manager recovery information.
- Save 2FA recovery codes outside the phone.
- Confirm you know your Apple Account password.
- Confirm trusted phone numbers and recovery contacts are current.
- Transfer authenticator apps using their official export or migration flow.
- If selling or giving away a device, sign out of Apple Account and erase it only after backup.

## First Boot

1. Update iOS or iPadOS.
2. Set a strong passcode.
3. Enable Face ID or Touch ID.
4. Sign in to Apple Account.
5. Confirm Find My is on.
6. Confirm iCloud Backup or encrypted computer backup is configured.
7. Update apps.
8. Review privacy prompts instead of tapping allow automatically.

## Security Baseline

- Use a six-digit or alphanumeric passcode, not a simple four-digit code.
- Turn on Stolen Device Protection.
- Keep two-factor authentication enabled.
- Use passkeys where available.
- Keep recovery contacts or a recovery key documented safely before enabling Advanced Data Protection.
- Keep Safari fraud warnings and security features enabled.
- Do not install configuration profiles unless you know exactly why they are needed.

## Privacy Baseline

- Review Location Services app by app.
- Use approximate location unless precision is needed.
- Review Photos access and prefer limited access for apps that only need one upload.
- Review Contacts, Bluetooth, Local Network, Microphone, Camera, and Motion permissions.
- Disable tracking prompts you do not want.
- Review Background App Refresh.
- Review notification previews on the lock screen.

## Useful Setup Choices

| Feature | Why it helps |
| :--- | :--- |
| Focus modes | Separate work, sleep, driving, and personal notifications. |
| Emergency SOS and Medical ID | Useful if the phone is locked during an emergency. |
| Passwords app / iCloud Keychain | Keeps passkeys and passwords synced across Apple devices. |
| Hide My Email | Limits exposure of your real email address. |
| iCloud Private Relay | Improves Safari browsing privacy for iCloud+ users where available. |
| Safety Check | Helps review sharing and account access. |

## Before Jailbreak Or Sideloading

- Finish normal setup first.
- Make an encrypted computer backup.
- Record iOS version, build number, model, and chip.
- Decide whether you care more about updates or preserving jailbreak eligibility.
- Do not install random profiles, certificates, or "jailbreak detectors" from ad-heavy sites.
