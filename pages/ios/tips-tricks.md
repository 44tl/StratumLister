# iOS Tips & Tricks

Useful iOS changes that do not require jailbreak.

## Security Wins

- Turn on Stolen Device Protection.
- Use a longer passcode.
- Use passkeys for accounts that support them.
- Review trusted devices under Apple Account settings.
- Use Safety Check to review sharing.
- Keep recovery contact or recovery key current.
- Use Lockdown Mode only when your threat level justifies the tradeoffs.

## Privacy Wins

- Use limited Photos access for apps that only need one image.
- Use approximate location for weather, shopping, and social apps.
- Turn off precise location for apps that do not need it.
- Review Local Network access.
- Review Bluetooth access.
- Review microphone and camera access after installing new apps.
- Use Hide My Email for newsletters, trials, and stores.
- Use iCloud Private Relay for Safari privacy if you have iCloud+ and it is available in your region.

## Notification Cleanup

- Turn off lock-screen previews for sensitive apps.
- Move low-value apps to Scheduled Summary if you use it.
- Use Focus modes for sleep, work, driving, and personal time.
- Allow calls from important contacts in Focus settings.
- Remove notification access from apps that abuse alerts.

## Battery And Performance

- Check Battery settings for apps with unexpected background use.
- Disable Background App Refresh for apps that do not need it.
- Use Low Power Mode intentionally, not permanently unless needed.
- Keep enough free storage for updates and caching.
- Avoid force-closing every app repeatedly; it usually does not help battery.
- Replace an aging battery instead of chasing cleanup apps.

## Storage Tips

- Use Photos optimization only if iCloud Photos is part of your plan.
- Delete old Messages attachments.
- Remove downloaded music, podcasts, maps, and streaming media you can redownload.
- Offload unused apps if you want to keep app documents.
- Export important files before deleting app data.

## Shortcuts And Automation

Good beginner automations:

- Low Battery Mode below a chosen battery level.
- Open a notes app when connecting to a specific car Bluetooth device.
- Change Watch face or Focus by time or location.
- Start a timer from NFC tags.
- Save clipboard text to a note, but avoid storing sensitive codes automatically.

Avoid shortcuts from random links unless you inspect every action.

## Hidden Checks

- Settings > General > About for carrier lock, parts history, model, and iOS version.
- Settings > Privacy & Security for permissions and app privacy controls.
- Settings > Battery for battery health and background activity.
- Settings > Apps for per-app settings.
- Settings > Apple Account for trusted devices, iCloud, Find My, and subscriptions.
