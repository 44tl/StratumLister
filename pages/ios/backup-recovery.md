# iOS Backup & Recovery

iOS recovery is easiest when you made the right backup before the problem. Use iCloud for convenience and encrypted computer backups for the most complete local restore path.

## Backup Types

| Backup | Best for | Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| iCloud Backup | Automatic daily backup | Easy restore path, depends on iCloud storage and account access. |
| Encrypted computer backup | Major updates, repairs, jailbreak work, local archive | Can include saved passwords, Wi-Fi settings, website history, Health data, and call history. |
| App-specific backup | Messengers, authenticators, notes, games | Many apps do not rely only on Apple backup. |
| Photos export | Long-term media safety | iCloud Photos sync is not the same as a separate archive. |

## Encrypted Backup Rules

Apple says encrypted local backups can include data that unencrypted backups do not, such as saved passwords, Wi-Fi settings, website history, Health data, and call history.

Rules:

- Use Finder on Mac, Apple Devices on Windows, or iTunes where required.
- Turn on Encrypt local backup before making the backup.
- Store the backup password in a password manager.
- Test that the backup exists.
- Do not lose the password; previous encrypted backups cannot be restored without it.

## Before Risky Work

Make an encrypted computer backup before:

- iOS updates on a critical device.
- Beta installs.
- Factory reset.
- Repair drop-off.
- Sideloading experiments.
- Jailbreak attempts.
- Tweak changes that affect SpringBoard, passcode, or system daemons.

Also export:

- Password manager recovery kit.
- 2FA recovery codes.
- Authenticator migration data.
- Signal or WhatsApp backups.
- Photos and important files outside iCloud if they matter.

## Lost Or Stolen Device

If the device is lost or stolen:

1. Use Find My or iCloud.com/find.
2. Mark the device as lost.
3. Do not remove it from your Apple Account while theft/loss recovery or insurance is active.
4. Change important passwords if the passcode may be known.
5. Watch Apple Account trusted devices and phone numbers.
6. Contact carrier for SIM/eSIM protection.

## Before Selling Or Giving Away

- Unpair Apple Watch if paired.
- Back up.
- Sign out of Apple Account.
- Turn off Find My as part of sign-out.
- Erase all content and settings.
- Confirm the device reaches the Hello setup screen.

Do not sell a device with Activation Lock still tied to your account.

## Jailbreak Recovery Notes

- Keep a clean backup before jailbreaking.
- Know whether your jailbreak is rootless, rootful, semi-untethered, or semi-tethered.
- Keep the tool version and install method noted.
- Remove suspicious tweaks before troubleshooting iOS itself.
- Restore root filesystem or use the jailbreak tool's uninstall process before updating when the tool documents that path.
