# Contributors

StratumLister is a collaborative effort. We rely on the technical expertise and vigilance of our community to maintain the highest standards of safety and accuracy.

<section class="contributors-page">
    <p class="contributors-lead">This project is maintained by Atlas and community contributors who improve the platform through source-backed technical guidance, bug reports, and documentation refinements.</p>
</section>

## What defines a contributor?
A Stratum contributor is anyone who has directly improved the project through our public repository. This includes:
- **Technical Writers**: Those who author new guides or refine existing ones.
- **Safety Auditors**: Users who report broken, suspicious, or outdated links.
- **Maintainers**: Those who review pull requests and verify the integrity of external resources.
- **Community Support**: Individuals who assist others in our community channels with troubleshooting.
